Apple Juice

As we know, an apple a day keeps the doctor away. The fruit is highly rich in fiber and the juice of apple contains pectin. This pectin is a soluble fiber which helps in trouble-free digestion. If you are suffering from severe constipation, a simple home remedy of drinking a glass of apple juice in the early morning can be an effective cure.

Beans

Beans are naturally high in fiber. Fiber helps to pass the stool and clear the tract. Several types of beans like, red beans, lima beans, black beans, navy beans, and garbanzo beans all the enriched with fiber. It will help to eliminate the problem of frequent constipation and gas.

Cabbage

Cabbage is rich in fiber which is very helpful in relieving constipation. You can eat cabbage as a salad, or you can drink the juice of it twice a day to ease constipation.
